Our Vision

In the service of convoy safety

By providing efficient and professional services, we contribute to the safety of trucking on the TruckersMP platform. These services must be experienced by our customers as a secure way to have a great convoy. The group was established the 11th of January 2015 by Volvotrebla. The starting point for this group was to develop the pilotage as a high-performance and operational company to achieve maximum synergy with other players in the community. Now, we have become famous for our convoy assistance!
"As a public entity we operate in accordance with international regulations & server rules with regard to the public safety."

Our services

We Always seek the right compromise. Good communications, understanding of all regulations and 3 Years of experience help us to find the right solutions.
Our members are experts in navigating and manoeuvering convoys. ﻿Every member is put through a series of severe tests including practical and theoretical tests. However, not everyone can access all functions. Some convoy functions require extra training to deliver a professional result. This makes that all our drivers are Always up to our standards.
We work with a system where everyone has it's own tasks. Here below we give you a simple view of what our services are and which tasks they have.
Convoy Leader
Convoy Leader his job is to drive at the front of the convoy at a steady speed.
Convoy Tail
Convoy Tail his job is to drive at the back of the convoy. This convoy function makes sure we Always know how much the convoy is spread out.
Pilot
A Pilot's job is to direct convoy participants in the right way. Pilots monitor the convoy constantly by overtaking.
Rapid Response Unit
A Rapid Response Unit's job is to supervise the convoy. If something goes wrong, this service reacts accordingly. It is a challenging task and therefore only carried out by Elite members.
